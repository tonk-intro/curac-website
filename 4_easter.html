Vacation Trips

Since 1945 the club has been leading vacation trips, usually to the UK National Parks (most commonly the Lakes, Peaks & Snowdonia) with the Easter Break being the most common time. These trips usually last 5-7 days and involve staying in one or more youth hostels. More history of the trips will be added to this page in time. There were no trips from 2018-2023 with a resurrection to the Easter Ramble in 2024 with a trip to the Hope Valley (peak district).

The 2024 Summer trip will be to the South Downs National Park in Sussex and will take place on the 15-20th September. The trip will involve 5-6 walks. If the trip is oversubscribed places will first go to those who filled out the pre-sign-up form which was sent out in June then the rest will be allocated by lot (trying not to split up people who ask to be together).
